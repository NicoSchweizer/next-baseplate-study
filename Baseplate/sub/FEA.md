### Finite Element Analysis (FEA)
* Divides a CAD model into discrete simple elements and generates a mesh
* Calculates the behavior of each element under certain conditions (e.g. forces, vibrations, thermal effects)
* Provides insights into the structural integrity, performance, and potential failure points of the design

## Modal Analysis
* Simulates every element as a harmonic oscillator
* Calculates the natural frequencies and mode shapes of the structure
